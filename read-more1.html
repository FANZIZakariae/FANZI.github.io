Definition of Smart systemes

A smart system is a type of technology system that is designed to be intelligent and capable of self-management. These systems incorporate advanced sensors, data analytics, and automation to make decisions, monitor conditions, and carry out actions without the need for human intervention.

Utilisations

Smart systems are used in a variety of applications and industries, including: Transportation, Energy, Healthcare, Manufacturing, Agriculture...

Additional Informations

The goal of a smart system is to improve efficiency, accuracy, and reliability while reducing costs and the risk of errors.
