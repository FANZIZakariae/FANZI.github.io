Definition of Web design

Web design refers to the process of creating the look, layout, and content of a website. It encompasses various elements such as color, typography, images, and user interface design to create an aesthetically pleasing and user-friendly experience for visitors to a website.

Utilisations

Web design has a wide range of uses and applications, some of which include: Corporate Websites, E-commerce Websites, Portfolio Websites, Blogs, Landing Pages, Educational Websites, Government Websites, Non-profit Website...

Additional Informations

The goal of web design is to create a website that effectively communicates a company's brand and message, while providing a positive user experience that encourages visitors to engage with the website and take a desired action, such as making a purchase or filling out a form.
